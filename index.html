This is a tetris implementation in HTML5.

It is almost compliant with the Tetris Guideline. T-spin recognition is nearly 3-corner T no kick, but will register on a triple.

It has three play modes as follows (each with its goal):

  • Marathon (score the most throughout the 15 levels)
  • Ultra (score the most in 3 minutes)
  • Sprint (clear 40 lines as fast as possible)

You can find the source code at github.com/clee704/tetris-html5.
